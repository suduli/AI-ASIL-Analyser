ASIL Reference Guide

Severity (S)

  • S0: No injuries
  • S1: Light to moderate injuries
  • S2: Severe to life-threatening injuries
  • S3: Life-threatening to fatal injuries

Exposure (E)

  • E0: Probability < 0.001%
  • E1: Probability 0.001% to 0.1%
  • E2: Probability 0.1% to 1%
  • E3: Probability 1% to 10%
  • E4: Probability > 10%

Controllability (C)

  • C0: Controllable in general
  • C1: Simply controllable
  • C2: Normally controllable
  • C3: Difficult to control or uncontrollable

ASIL Levels

  • QM: Quality Management - No safety requirements
  • ASIL A: Lowest integrity level
  • ASIL B: Medium-low integrity level
  • ASIL C: Medium-high integrity level
  • ASIL D: Highest integrity level
